Rooms & Services

Padraic Hostel offers you different room types
The larger rooms are perfect for big group trips so that everyone can stay together, while the smaller rooms are great for couples or a handful of friends on a backpacking trip. You can book these rooms individually or as a group.

Bar & Food

Our kitchen is equipped with every little thing you need to prepare a delicious meal. After breakfast you can cook your own food, keep your groceries in the fridge, or just serve yourself from our fresh baked coffee. Also we have free tea and chocolate for milk available all day.

Events

Many other things are pre arranged for you from our hostel, but in a seasonal basis. In the summer, we help you organize for the music festivals, surf camps and classes, or just visits to the beach. In the winter we organize more indoors activities, as it feels better to be inside in the warmth. Also special requests on demand are many times pre-arranged.
